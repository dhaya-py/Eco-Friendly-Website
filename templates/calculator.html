How It Works

Our calculator estimates your carbon footprint based on standard emission factors:

  • Electricity: 0.85 kg CO2 per kWh
  • Natural Gas: 2.3 kg CO2 per therm

These are approximate values. Your actual footprint may vary based on specific factors.
